# Privacy Policy for Claude Usage Pro

**Last Updated:** January 20, 2025

## Overview

Claude Usage Pro is a browser extension that helps you track your Claude.ai usage. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Usage statistics** scraped from your Claude.ai settings page (session %, weekly %)
- **Draft token counts** calculated locally as you type
- **Settings preferences** (display options, feature toggles)

### What We DO NOT Collect
- Your conversations with Claude
- Personal information
- Browsing history outside Claude.ai
- Any data for analytics or advertising

## Data Storage

### Local Storage (Default)
All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device unless you enable Firebase sync.

### Firebase Sync (Optional)
If you choose to enable cross-device sync:
- You provide your OWN Firebase credentials
- Data is stored in YOUR Firebase account
- We have no access to your Firebase data
- You can delete your data at any time

### Anthropic API (Optional)
If you provide an Anthropic API key for accurate token counting:
- Only the text you're drafting is sent to Anthropic
- This is sent directly to Anthropic, not through us
- Used solely for token counting

## Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties.

## Your Rights

- All your data is under your control
- You can clear extension data at any time via Chrome settings
- Uninstalling the extension removes all local data

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

## Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be noted with an updated date.
